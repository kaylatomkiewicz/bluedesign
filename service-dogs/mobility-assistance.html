Mobility Assistance Dog

Mobility Assistance Dogs are trained to help their owners get around and accomplish things on their own. These dogs are valuable assets for someone returning to the work force or school because they decrease dependence on other people. Mobility Assistance Dogs can help people with a variety of disabilities including spinal cord injury, brain injury, muscular dystrophy, arthritis, and many more.

Mobility Assistance Dogs are trained to help their owners in many ways including (but not limited to):

  • Retrieving dropped items,
  • Bringing the phone,
  • Turning lights on or off,
  • Opening doors at home,
  • Pressing elevator buttons,
  • Assist with dressing,
  • Pulling a wheelchair up inclines and ramps.
